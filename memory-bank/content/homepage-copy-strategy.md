# Homepage Copy Strategy: Inkdex as Instagram Search Layer

## Core Messaging Framework

### Primary Value Proposition
**"The search engine for tattoo artists on Instagram"**

We're not another portfolio platform. We're the layer that makes Instagram's massive tattoo content searchable by style, vibe, and visual similarity.

### Key Message Points

1. **For Users:**
   - Instagram has millions of tattoo posts, but finding YOUR artist is impossible
   - We built a visual search layer that actually works
   - Search here → Find artists → Connect on Instagram

2. **For Artists:**
   - We auto-curate your Instagram into a searchable portfolio
   - Claim your free profile or upgrade for premium features
   - Get discovered by clients searching your exact style

3. **The Bridge:**
   - We don't replace Instagram—we make it discoverable
   - Think Google Images, but for tattoo artists

## Tone & Voice Guidelines

### Voice Characteristics:
- **Direct, not clever**: Say what we do in plain language
- **Confident, not hype-y**: We solve a real problem
- **Helpful, not salesy**: We're a utility, not a destination

### Avoid:
- ❌ "Revolutionary platform"
- ❌ "Game-changing technology"
- ❌ "Join our community"
- ❌ Generic startup jargon

### Use:
- ✅ "Search layer"
- ✅ "We scan Instagram"
- ✅ "Find artists who match"
- ✅ "Connect on Instagram"

---

## Copy Principles

### 1. Be Explicit About Instagram
Don't hide that we're a layer on top of Instagram. It's our strength:
- "We search Instagram so you don't have to"
- "Find artists on Instagram by style, not hashtags"
- "Connect directly on their Instagram profile"

### 2. Address the Pain Point
Instagram search for tattoos genuinely sucks:
- Hashtag spam (#tattoo shows everything and nothing)
- Location search is too broad
- No visual similarity search
- No style filtering that actually works

### 3. Clarify the Flow
Users need to understand they're NOT staying on Inkdex:
- Search happens here
- Results show artist matches
- Click-through goes to Instagram
- Booking/contact happens there

### 4. Artist Value = Discoverability
Artists don't need another portfolio platform. They need clients:
- "Get discovered by clients searching your style"
- "Your Instagram becomes searchable"
- "Show up when someone searches for your aesthetic"

## Open Questions for Discussion

1. **Instagram Branding**: Do we need Instagram's permission to say "Search Instagram"?
   - **Answer**: Likely no (we're describing what we do, not claiming affiliation)
   - **Safe alternative**: "Search tattoo artists who post on Instagram"

2. **Artist Claiming**: Should free vs paid be on homepage or separate landing page?
   - **Recommendation**: Mention claiming on homepage, details on `/artists/claim`

3. **Social Proof**: Do we have metrics to share?
   - 1,501 artists indexed
   - 9,803 portfolio images searchable
   - 8 cities covered

4. **Pain Point Emphasis**: How hard should we lean into "Instagram search sucks"?
   - **Recommendation**: Neutral positioning ("searching by hashtag gives chaos")
   - **Avoid**: Negative framing ("Instagram's search is terrible")
